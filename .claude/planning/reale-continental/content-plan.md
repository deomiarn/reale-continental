## Notes for Content Writers

### Writing Style
- **Sentence length**: 12-18 words average (German allows longer sentences, but keep readable)
- **Active voice**: 80%+ (e.g., "Wir reparieren Ihr Auto" not "Ihr Auto wird repariert")
- **Technical terms**: Use when appropriate (Karosserie Instandsetzung, Schadensgutachten) but explain context
- **Avoid**: Marketing fluff, superlatives without proof (e.g., "weltbeste Werkstatt"), generic claims

### Keyword Integration Tips
- **H1**: Must include primary keyword, but read naturally (not "Autoreparaturen Dielsdorf Autoreparaturen Dielsdorf")
- **First 100 words**: Include primary keyword once, secondary keyword once
- **Synonyms**: Use LSI keywords to avoid repetition (e.g., "Karosserie Instandsetzung" instead of always "Karosserieschäden")
- **Location mentions**: Don't force "Dielsdorf" into every sentence; use "Region Zürich", "Umgebung", "lokal"

### E-E-A-T Content Requirements
- **Experience**: Mention years (5+, 15+), number of repairs (if data available), customer testimonials
- **Expertise**: Use precise technical terms, explain processes in detail (4-step workflow), mention certifications if any
- **Authority**: Reference insurance partnerships, quality standards, industry affiliations
- **Trust**: Show real contact info (Luigi's email, direct phone), transparent pricing mentions, guarantees

### Conversion-Focused Writing
- **CTAs**: Action verbs (Vereinbaren, Anrufen, Kontaktieren), urgency without pressure ("Jetzt" not "Sofort bevor es zu spät ist")
- **Benefits over features**: "Keine Vorleistung" (benefit) not just "Direktabrechnung" (feature)
- **Proof points**: Testimonials, statistics, process transparency
- **Friction reduction**: "Kostenlose Beratung", "Unverbindlich", "Schnelle Rückmeldung"
