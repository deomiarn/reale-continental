## Appendix: Keyword Density Calculator

**Formula:** (Keyword Count / Total Word Count) × 100 = Density %

**Example (Homepage, 700 words):**
- "Autoreparaturen Dielsdorf": 10 mentions → (10/700) × 100 = 1.43% ✅
- "Karosserieschäden": 7 mentions → (7/700) × 100 = 1% ✅
- "Frontscheibenreparatur": 7 mentions → (7/700) × 100 = 1% ✅
- Total primary keywords: 24 mentions → (24/700) × 100 = 3.43% ✅ (safe range: 2-5%)

**Warning Thresholds:**
- <0.5%: Likely under-optimized
- 0.5-1.5%: Optimal range
- 1.5-2.5%: Good, but monitor for naturalness
- >3%: Risk of over-optimization (keyword stuffing penalty)
